What is this site?

{{ site_name }} is a free fantasy prediction game for the FIFA World Cup 2026 in Canada, Mexico, and the United States. Pick outcomes for group-stage matches, earn points from correct answers, compete on leaderboards, and optionally use AI Predict to fill predictions before kickoff when you enable it on your profile.

This is not real-money gambling or betting. There are no cash prizes — only points, rankings, and bragging rights.

How it works

  1. Create an account (email or Google sign-in).
  2. Complete onboarding: display name, favorite team, timezone.
  3. Submit predictions before each match kicks off.
  4. Use up to five point boosters on group-stage matches (2× points when applied).
  5. Track results on the dashboard, scorecards, and leaderboard.

AI Predict

If you turn on AI Predict, the site can automatically submit predictions for you within a configurable window before kickoff (default: two hours). Answers may use Google Gemini when configured, with a heuristic fallback. You can change this anytime in your profile. See Privacy Policy for how data is used.

Advertising

We may show Google AdSense display advertisements on some public pages to help cover hosting costs. Ads are optional and controlled by site configuration. Advertising cookies are described in our Privacy Policy.
